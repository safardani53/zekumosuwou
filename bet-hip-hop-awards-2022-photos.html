The BET Hip Hop Awards, which will honor the best of the best in hip hop music, will air on Oct. 4, 2022 after being pre-taped on Sept. 30. The event will be star-studded, per usual. Up for Best Live Performer are Cardi B, Doja Cat, Drake, J. Cole, Kanye West, Kendrick Lamar, and Tyler, the Creator. Meanwhile, Cardi B, Doja Cat, Drake, Future, Kanye, Kendrick, and Megan Thee Stallion are up for Hip Hop Artist of the Year. Plus, there will be 40 artists performing to bring the hottest hip hop beats to the stage.

Of course, an award show is not complete without fashion, and this year’s red carpet walkers certainly brought their A-game. For instance, Remy Ma, seen above, turned heads on the red carpet in a velvet green catsuit from D’Anthony Designz with a unique U-shaped cape that came around her chest and draped to the ground from the back of her arms.
