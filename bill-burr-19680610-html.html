Bill Burr (Comedian) - Age, Birthday, Bio, Facts, Family, Net Worth, Height &amp; More

William Frederick Burr is an American comedian, actor, and writer. He has released multiple stand-up comedy specials, most notably Why Do I Do This?, Let It Go, You People Are All the Same, I'm Sorry You Feel That Way, Walk Your Way Out, and Paper Tiger. The latter received a nomination for Best Comedy Album at the 63rd Annual Grammy Awards. Burr also created, co-wrote, and voiced the lead character in the Netflix animated sitcom F Is for Family. His other notable acting roles include Patrick Kuby in the AMC series Breaking Bad, Migs Mayfeld in the Disney+ series The Mandalorian, and Ray Bishop in the film The King of Staten Island. He has hosted the twice-weekly Monday Morning Podcast since 2007 and co-founded the All Things Comedy network in 2012.
