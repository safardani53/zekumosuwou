Kaity Tong's Early Life & Education

Kaity Tong was born on 23rd July 1947, in Qingdao, China. She is the one and only daughter of George Tong and Anita Tong. Tong arrived in the United States with her family at the age of four.

Although she was born in China, she holds American nationality. She belongs to Asian ethnicity and her birth sign is Cancer.

Talking about Kaity Tong's education, she attended Bryn Mawr College and later graduated in honors with a bachelor of arts degree in English literature. 

Furthermore, she was accepted to the doctoral program in Chinese and Japanese literature at Stanford University. She also managed to complete her master's degree in Asian studies while working in the news business.
