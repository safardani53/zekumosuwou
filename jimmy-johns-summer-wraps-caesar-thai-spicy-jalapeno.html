Popular sandwich chain and Subway rival launches new summer menu and brings back fan favorite

A SANDWICH spot has brought back wraps for summer, including a brand-new item.

Jimmy John's debuted the Jalapeño Ranch Chicken Wrap alongside two classics.

The new, spicy wrap features chicken, provolone cheese, jalapeño ranch, crispy jalapeños, and red pepper flakes in a flour wrap.

Back by popular demand are the Chicken Caesar Wrap and the Thai Chicken Wrap.

The Caesar wrap comes with chicken, parmesan cheese, Caesar dressing, and mini croutons in a garlic and herb wrap.

The Thai Chicken Wrap has chicken, Thai satay sauce, and crispy carrot noodles in a flour wrap.

All the wraps are $12.50.

“At Jimmy John’s, we are known for our fresh, made-to-order sandwiches and are constantly innovating to bring our customers bold and unexpected sandwich experiences,” said Dustin Hilinski, Director of Culinary Innovation at Jimmy John’s.

“That’s why we have raised the bar this summer by offering three delicious wraps, each with unique flavor combinations, including the new Jalapeño Ranch Chicken Wrap, perfect for those who crave a little kick in each bite.”

Freaky Fast Rewards members, who order all three wraps between June 5 and June 11, will earn the Wrap Royalty Achievement Badge in the app.

JIMMY JOHN'S NEWEST ITEMS

The sandwich shop has been beefing up its menu this month.

Jimmy John's introduced a fudge brownie as a third dessert option alongside the oatmeal raisin and chocolate chip cookies.

Diners were over the moon about the new option, which features a mix of semi-sweet chocolate, cocoa, and melted chocolate.

On Twitter, one person wrote: "I was so happy to see this on the menu when I ordered my Turkey Tommy today. Thank you so much for these magical creations."

After seeing the advertisement for the brownies, another customer tweeted: "Why do they have to keep playing this commercial, I want one so bad!"

Plus, three salads are being tested at select locations in Wisconsin.

There is the Baja Chicken Salad, which comes with provolone cheese, chili lime seasoning, crispy jalapenos, Jimmy peppers, smashed avocado, tomato, and onion all on a bed of lettuce blend topped with jalapeno ranch dressing.

Next is the Chicken Bacon Ranch Salad, featuring bacon, parmesan, croutons, cucumber, and tomato on a lettuce blend topped with ranch dressing.

Finally, as a nod to some of its most popular sandwiches, the Italian Salad consists of salami, capicola, provolone, cucumber, oregano, basil, croutons, tomato, and onion on a lettuce blend with classic Italian vinaigrette dressing.

The salads aren't on the official menu yet, instead, diners can ask for an unwich - which swaps the bread for a lettuce wrap.
