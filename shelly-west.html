Shelly West is a famous Country Singer. She was born on May 23, 1958 and her birthplace is Cleveland, OH. Shelly is also well known as, 1980s country singer who sang the #1 singles “Jose Cuervo” and “You’re the Reason God Made Oklahoma.”

Shelly is originated from United States. She is the daughter of country star Dottie West.
