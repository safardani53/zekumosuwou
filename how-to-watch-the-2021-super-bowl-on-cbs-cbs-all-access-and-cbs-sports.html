How To Watch The 2021 Super Bowl On CBS, CBS All Access, And CBS Sports

Stream Super Bowl LV, including the halftime show, live on Sunday, Feb. 7, 2021 at 6 PM ET / 3 PM PT with CBS All Access and on CBS Sports.

Super Bowl LV is coming to Raymond James Stadium in Tampa, Florida—and to whatever screen you prefer just about anywhere you are!

Here's what you need to know about how to watch the 2021 edition of the big game between the Kansas City Chiefs and Tampa Bay Buccaneers. Additional details may be announced at a later date, so check back here as this page will be updated when more information becomes available.

What time is Super Bowl LV?
This year, the Super Bowl is on Sunday, Feb. 7, 2021. Traditional festivities like the opening national anthem and coin toss start streaming online at 6 PM ET / 3 PM PT with kickoff about half an hour later. CBS is serving up seven hours of pregame coverage, so check out the full schedule below—and remember you'll want to catch postgame highlights, too.

What channel is Super Bowl LV on?
CBS is carrying the Super Bowl on stations live across the nation (see the full CBS schedule here) as well as streaming live on CBS All Access and on CBS Sports.

How can I watch Super Bowl LV online?
You can stream the 2021 Super Bowl live with CBS All Access and on CBS Sports.

SIGN UP: Stream Super Bowl LV, The Halftime Show, And More On CBS All Access

How can I watch the Pepsi Super Bowl LV Halftime Show?
While you're watching the Super Bowl, just stay tuned to CBS, CBS All Access, or CBS Sports to catch the Pepsi Super Bowl LV Halftime Show. GRAMMY®️ Award-winning The Weeknd, known for hits like "Blinding Lights" and "The Hills," will be headlining the can't-miss spectacular.

What's the Super Bowl LV pregame schedule?
With seven hours of pregame coverage, CBS has you (and everything!) covered. The following times are ET.

How can I watch the best Super Bowl commercials of 2021?
Aside from enjoying the blockbuster commercials during the Super Bowl itself, CBS has a new 2021 edition of Super Bowl Greatest Commercials airing Wednesday, Feb. 3 at 8/7 on CBS and CBS All Access. The interactive countdown special allows you to vote for the top commercial of the past 20 Super Bowls.

What else is happening on CBS after Super Bowl LV?
You don't want to miss the big game—but you also don't want to miss what comes on after!

Immediately following the Super Bowl is the premiere of Queen Latifah's new drama The Equalizer (10 PM ET / 7 PM PT; time is approximate after post-game coverage). In the reimagining of the classic series, Latifah stars as Robyn McCall, an enigmatic woman with a mysterious background who uses her extensive skills to help those with nowhere else to turn.

The big evening concludes with a special Sunday edition of the #1 show in late night, The Late Show with Stephen Colbert (11:35 PM ET / 8:35 PM PT; time is approximate following your late local news).

Plus, there will be continued coverage of the big game on CBS Sports. 

Who's playing in Super Bowl LV?
The 2021 Super Bowl will pit AFC Champions, the Kansas City Chiefs, against the NFC Champions, the Tampa Bay Buccaneers. 

What devices can I use to watch Super Bowl LV on CBS All Access?
You can watch CBS All Access on online at CBS.com, on mobile devices via the CBS app for iOS and Android, and on Roku Players, Apple TV, Chromecast, Android TV, Xbox One, PlayStation 4, Fire TV, Samsung Smart TVs, Comcast Flex, Facebook Portal, Vizio Smartcast TVs, LG Smart TVs, Amazon Prime Video Channels, and Apple TV channels.

Or, stream the big game on CBS Sports!

What else do I get with CBS All Access besides streaming the 2021 Super Bowl?
CBS All Access, ViacomCBS' existing subscription video on-demand and live streaming service, will be rebranded as Paramount+ on March 4. Whenever you sign up, you can look forward to a growing roster of live sports and loads of entertainment.

For sports fans, there are marquee events like SEC on CBS, college basketball (including March Madness with the Final Four and championship game), elite soccer (including UEFA Champions League, UEFA Europa League, and National Women's Soccer League, and professional golf (including the PGA Tour, the Masters, and the PGA Championship).

You'll also get access to an ever-growing collection of exclusive original series from CBS All Access that you won't find elsewhere. These currently include the critically acclaimed hit The Good Fight, The Twilight Zone, Star Trek: Picard, Tooning Out The News, No Activity, Why Women Kill, The Thomas John Experience, and more.

Of course, you can stream all current CBS Primetime, Late Night, and Daytime shows. Those current primetime series include NCIS, FBI, SEAL Team, All Rise, Young Sheldon, and The Neighborhood, plus upcoming new series like The Equalizer and Clarice. You'll also get every prior season of 14 hit CBS series on demand, including Survivor, NCIS, and Hawaii Five-0

If classics are more your style, CBS All Access offers all of the Star Trek television series, acclaimed hits like Cheers, Perry Mason, and so many others!

The expanding services recently added hit shows from more networks in the ViacomCBS portfolio like BET, Comedy Central, MTV, Nickelodeon, Smithsonian Channel, TV Land, and VH1.
