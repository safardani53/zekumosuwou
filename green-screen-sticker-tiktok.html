What is the green screen sticker on TikTok?

The green screen sticker is another fun effect that's available in the TikTok editing section of the app. Unlike the green screen filter, which allows the user to adjust and change their background, the green screen sticker allows the user to place a separate image or video within their video, or the opposite ‚Äî place a video of themselves within a still image or video. 

TikTok released this feature on the app around the beginning of June and featured a video showcasing its use on Facebook.

In this video, the green screen sticker is how the user gets the Google search engine photo to show up on the screen.
