Who is Vinny Appice:

Vinny Appice is a famous Drummer. He was born on September 13, 1957 and his birthplace is Brooklyn, NY. Vinny is also well known as, Heavy metal drummer who has played with Dio, Big Noize, Heaven & Hell, and Black Sabbath.

Vinny is originated from United States. He met John Lennon when he was sixteen years old and went on to appear on a few of his recordings.
