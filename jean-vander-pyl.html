Who is Jean Vander Pyl:

Jean Vander Pyl is a famous Voice Actress. She was born on October 11, 1919 and her birthplace is Philadelphia, PA. Jean is also well known as, Voice actress who is predominantly recognized for having voiced The Flintstones character Wilma Flintstone. On the show she was also the credited voice over artist for Pebbles Flintstone and she also voiced Rosie the robot maid on The Jetsons.

Jean is originated from United States. Both she and Alan Reed lent their voices to The Flintstones.
