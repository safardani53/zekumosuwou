Iskra Lawrence is Out to Destroy the Thigh Gap Myth

Does she have a thigh gap or does she not have a thigh gap?

To Iskra Lawrence, this is not the question.

On April 29, the model shared a pair of photos on Instagram, posing both times in the same outfit on the same day and sharing the images side-by-side.

What point was she trying to make?

We’ll let the 25-year old beauty explain:

“No thigh gap or thigh gap who cares.

"I’ll be the first one to tell you pics are all about good lightening and angles.

"Always remember social medias not real life never let anyone else’s pics make you feel insecure about yourself.”

Take a look below at the photos in question. She’s sending a pretty good message here, no?

Celebrities around the country have sadly gotten caught up in the whole thigh gap craze.

Kim Zolciak, for example, insists that hers is real.

Demi Lovato, meanwhile, once cursed her thigh gap off.

"You are meant to be YOU no one else, your body is your home so love and respect it. When you look after it beautiful things happens," Lawrence added as a caption to the pictures above.

Last month, the face of the #AerieREAL campaign (which strives to promote body positivity) rose to viral stardom on when she shut down a troll by sharing a video of herself clad in lingerie … and eating a bag of potato chips.

"This is for anyone who has ever been called FAT. Thanks for the inspirational words," she wrote at the time.

Due to her sizeable rear end, Lawrence has been compared to Kim Kardashian.

But could you ever imagine Kim Kardashian making these sort of positive body type of comments?

Sadly, neither can we.
