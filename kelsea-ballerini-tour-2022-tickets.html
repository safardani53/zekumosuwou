Kelsea Ballerini tour 2022: How can I buy tickets?

Kelsea Ballerini is gearing up for the release of her new album, Subject To Change, which is scheduled to drop on September 23, 2022.

In honor of the release, the country sensation is embarking on a 10-night-only tour to perform some of the new tracks.

Presale tickets go on sale on June 26 and can be purchased through Ballerini's website or on Ticketmaster.

Who is Kelsea Ballerini?

Ballerini is a country pop singer who was born on September 12, 1993, in Mascot, Tennessee.

She began her music career by writing music as a child and went on to sign with Black River Entertainment in 2014.

Since then, she has released four studio albums and several hit songs, including Miss Me More, Peter Pan, Half of My Hometown, and Roses.

Since 2017, she has been married to Australian country music singer and songwriter Morgan Evans.
