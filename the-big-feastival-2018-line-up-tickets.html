When does The Big Feastival Start? How can I get tickets?

The festival runs from August 24-26, 2018

For the full list of opening hours over the course of the festival, visit the official website.

Tickets are still available at a range of different prices from The Big Feastival website.

All children must have a valid entry ticket to enter the event.

12 and under tickets must be booked at the time of adult purchase.

Free child day tickets are limited to two per adult.

Where is The Big Feastival 2018 taking place?

The Big Feastival 2018 site is on Alex James’ Farm near Kingham, Oxfordshire, OX7 6UJ.

Kingham is a village in the Cotswolds, and is located roughly four miles southwest of Chipping Norton.

It's easy to get to by coach, rail or by car with direct trains running from London Paddington to Kingham train station.

There's more information on the venue at the festival's website.

You can also follow the festival on Twitter and Instagram.
