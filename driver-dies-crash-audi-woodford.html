Horror as driver dies after smashing Audi into building in London as police launch probe

A DRIVER has died after crashing his Audi into a building in London.

Police were scrambled to the High Road in South Woodford this morning after the horror unfolded.

Sadly the male driver could not be saved and was declared dead at the scene shortly after.

Shocking pictures show the front of the Audi TT crumpled against a brick wall.

A probe has now been launched but it is understood no other vehicles were involved.

Officers are now investigating the circumstances of the crash.
