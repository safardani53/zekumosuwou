Miley has a habit of posting racy photos on Instagram. It’s kind of her thing. Some of Miley’s fans are coming to her defense over the photos. User sabi.hernandez commented, “I don’t think she meant any harm posting this pic tbh.” Another wrote, “Lmao everyones so mad at this but all she said was mood?? Its not like she took the pics.”
