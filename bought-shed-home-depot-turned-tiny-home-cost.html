I bought a shed from Home Depot and turned it into a stunning tiny home - it cost less than $7,000 &amp;

A MAN has revealed how he turned a shed he purchased from a popular hardware store into a nice tiny house for less than $10,000.

The TikTok user, which shared a detailed video about how he turned a shed from Home Depot into a stunning pint-sized home, has more than 40,000 followers who enjoy the tiny-house content.

The account known as Tiny Tok (@minihomemonster) on the app shared the tiny homeowner's clip with the caption, asking its followers: "Would you live here?!"

Tiny Tok included hashtags in the caption like #homedepot, #tinyhousetour, #tinyhousebuild, #tinyhousemovement and #tinyhomes.

The video began with the older guy wearing a hat while standing inside a bare shed while explaining what he likes about the specific dwelling and why.

"As you can see, the tough shed comes with the basic essentials but not everything," he explained.

More on tiny homes

"It does come with a better floor, better door, though I changed the door out to an outside house door," The tiny home owner went on to say.

"Better walls, and better roofing was important to me because I want this tough shed to last a long time."

He said that the shed has a 50-year guarantee.

"So, I'll be long dead before this thing falls apart," he added.

The video switched to show clips of him installing electricity into the soon-to-be tiny home.

Then he said: "We bought some Aqua Guard engineering wood flooring."

"That's kind of beautiful and easy to put in," the man stated.

At the end, he stood proudly inside the tiny house that had two floors, windows and a door.

"I'm standing in here, in my d-i-y bunkhouse or cabin," the guy said.

"It's just basically a tough shed that we converted into a bunkhouse and last weekend, we went ahead and put in this banister and this railing over here on the stairs."

The happy tiny-house owner showed that the fan in the small house worked, as well as the light switches.

"It must be good," he exclaimed.

The final shot in the video revealed the outside of his stunning tiny home with a porch as the snow fell.

The video has been viewed almost 850,000 times on TikTok.

Answering the question presented in the caption about people's opinions on living in the pint-sized home, a TikToker said: "Yep sure would without hesitation."

A user asked: "Final price tag?"

Someone answered their question: "I believe it runs around 10k from Home Depot. It's less expensive to buy the materials and do it yourself. Around 2-3k. Finished for 5-7."

Another person said: "I've been wanting a tiny home of any sort just don't know where to start and stuff."

A different TikTok user added: "Awesome! I'd like to do this some day!!"
