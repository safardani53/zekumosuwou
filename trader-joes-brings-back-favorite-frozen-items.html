Trader Joe's brings back $4 favorite frozen item - and fans are 'really happy' about it

FALL is just around the corner, and Trader Joe's is bringing back its seasonal selection of delicious snacks, frozen meals, and produce.

One Trader Joe's enthusiast (@traderjoestalia) has shared a rundown on the highly anticipated product drops that could be coming to stores near you.

Talia dashed into the supermarket with a singular mission in mind, finding the newest flavors for fall.

She began in the sauce section, picking out one of her favorite items.

"I've been so excited for this product to hit the shelves," she explained in her video.

"I've known about it for about three weeks. It's Red Boat's Vietnamese simmer sauce."

Read more on Trader Joe's

Talia added that she was a big fan of the “Red Boat” brand when it came to fish sauces.

Once she was satisfied with her sauce selection, the eager shopper moved to the frozen section.

"A returning frozen item I'm really happy about, the Root Vegetable Fries," she noted. "These are really pretty. All the colors are really nice, and it reminds me of Fall."

The bag contains a healthy medley of roasted sweet potatoes, carrots, beets, and parsnips.

"Another returning item was the Tri-colored Mini Gnocci," she said, picking up a bag.

"I really like the texture of this one. Super quick. Super easy. I throw some chicken sausage in there."

Talia also mentioned the Langostino Tails but added that they were a bit out of her budget.

"I don't know if this is for everybody, but this has been gone from my Traders Joe's for a while, and it's back. Not that it really matters to me because I can't afford it for $14.99, but if you can, it's back!"

The last feature was given to one of Trader Joe's staple microwave meals.

"Another new product that is back that I am absolutely loving right now is the Broccoli and Cheddar Quiche. I can warm it up in the microwave in three minutes, and it's super easy."

Many of Talia's TikTok followers were excited to learn about the new products, and also eager to find out if their other favorites would be making a comeback.

"That sauce is bomb. I like it with shrimp." one person commented.
