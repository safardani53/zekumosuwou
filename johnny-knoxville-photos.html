Photos of the Actor &amp; Stunt Performer Hollywood Life

Johnny Knoxville, 50, is an actor, comedian, filmmaker, and stunt performer, best known for co-creating the series Jackass which aired for three seasons on MTV from 2000 to 2002. The show featured a wild cast of characters who pulled off outrageous stunts and pranks, often in public where onlookers watched in amazement and disbelief. The show went on to become a major movie franchise, pumping out three feature films, Jackass: The Movie, Jackass Number Two, and Jackass 3D. The forthcoming flick, Jackass Forever, is set to release February 4, 2022.

In addition to his Jackass days, Johnny has acted in several films and TV shows, such as Men in Black II, Coyote Ugly, The Dukes of Hazzard, and more. Johnny was married to Melanie Lynn Cates in 1995 and they had a daughter, Madison, in 1996. After separating in 2009, Johnny married Naomi Nelson in 2010.
