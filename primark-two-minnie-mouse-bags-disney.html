Primark has released two new Minnie Mouse bags from 8... and Disney fans are going wild for them

ATTENTION Disney lovers! Primark has released two Minnie Mouse backpacks, and shoppers are going wild for them.

The high street chain shared pictures of two bags, priced from £8, and they have received thousands of likes on Instagram in under 24 hours.

The pale pink bags come with red bows featuring the trademark polka dot design, and look perfect for the Mouseketeer in your life.

The smaller of the two, which comes with a shoulder strap, costs £8.

Meanwhile the pink, larger backpack is a purse-friendly £12, and looks way more expensive than its modest price tag.

Both of the Disney bags are currently on sale across the UK, and excited shoppers couldn’t contain their excitement.

One fan wrote: “So pretty!”

Another spoke of an upcoming trip to the famous attraction and said: “I think I may need more bags for Disney!”

Meanwhile one shopper gushed: “Omg these are so cute.”

More on Primark

It isn’t the first Primark product that fans have gone wild for.

They recently unveiled their Mickey Mouse onesies as well as a version featuring Chip from Beauty And The Beast.
