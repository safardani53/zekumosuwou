A former bikini Olympia competitor, Courtney King, recently celebrated her 30th birthday this September.

With a long caption, she posted how she felt leaving her 20s and entering a new age. She said, “My goal this new year is to embrace where I am. to embrace change. to take in the present moment because it’s the only moment we have. here is to 30. The best is yet to come.

Courtney King, a professional athlete in the IFBB, won the Bikini Olympia championship in 2016, making her one of the most important figures in the history of the Bikini division.

King’s excellent performance in 2016 was a significant milestone, setting the division on a new path.

She stood out from the competition thanks to her great physical condition and her well-developed leg muscles.

Since leaving the Bikini fitness contest, Courtney has been spending time with her fiance and hosting a podcast with her teammate.

Courtney King Age And Wikipedia

Courtney King was born on September 13, 1993, in Chicago, IL. She turned 30 earlier this year.

During her childhood, she actively participated in various sports, such as soccer, gymnastics, and basketball.

Additionally, she was part of the cross-country and swimming teams. Regular visits to the gym helped her maintain her fitness levels and enhance her physical abilities.

Her passion for fitness led her to discover bikini competitions, which captivated her interest.

Later, a woman at the gym noticed King’s impressive physique and inquired if she competed in the Bikini division.

King made her professional debut in 2013 at the Battle on the Beach Pro, securing the runner-up position.

This success was followed by victories at the Europa Games Pro and Tampa Pro, which enabled her qualification for the Bikini Olympia competition.

Her commendable seventh-place finish at the 2013 Bikini Olympia contest marked a promising start to her professional career.

Courtney Is Engaged To Chris Bumstead

Courtney, a former bodybuilder, is happily engaged to her fiancé Chris Bumstead. The couple first met at the 2017 Mr. Olympia competition, and Courtney initiated contact with Chris in 2018 by sending him a direct message.

Since then, their relationship has thrived, and they have been dating for many years. Finally, in 2022, Chris proposed to Courtney in Sedona, Arizona.

Courtney shared their engagement picture and wrote a caption expressing her love for Chris, saying, “Christopher Adam Bumstead, you are the best thing that’s ever happened to me. I feel like the luckiest woman alive knowing that I get to marry you!! My heart is yours until the end of time.”

They are now recognized as one of the most adored couples in the bodybuilding community.

Chris Bumstead Is A Canadian Body Builder

Like his finance, Chris is also a bodybuilder who is widely known as Cbum in the bodybuilding world.

Cbum is a renowned IFBB professional bodybuilder who has achieved remarkable success in the Classic Physique category, winning five consecutive Mr. Olympia Classic Physique titles from 2019 to 2023.

At the 2023 Olympia, Chris was crowned the Classic Physique Olympia champion for the fifth time in a row, having previously won the title every year since 2019.
