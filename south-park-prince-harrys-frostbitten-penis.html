South Park fans left 'crying with laughter' as show rinses Prince Harry's frostbitten penis

SOUTH Park fans have been left in hysterics at the show's comical take on Prince Harry and Meghan Markle - and have even rinsed his 'frostbitten penis'.

The adult animated comedy aired a fictional episode that appeared to directly mock Meghan Markle and husband Prince Harry as the characters endured a bit to gain total privacy having been working members of the Canadian Royal Family.

The programme aimed a particular hard-hitting dig at the Prince's 'blue penis' which appeared to be a clear reference to the Royal's frostbitten penis that he wrote about in excruciating detail in his memoir, Spare.

In one scene, having settled as a privacy seeking couple in South Park, the Prince and Princess are left appalled to discover that their new home has been plastered in magazine and newspaper cuttings featuring their faces, with one in particular appearing to be a resemblance to Meghan's highly publicised interview in The Cut.

As Kyle confronted the Royals, the furious Princess responded: "He victimised me. It's because I'm an ethnic woman!"

The ginger haired Prince then remarked: "This is an outrage, we will just see how he deals with my blue penis."

The royal could then be seen exposing his blue penis outside Kyle's window in a bid for attention but Kyle did not hear or see the Prince rubbing his penis up and down.

Twitter went into meltdown over the moment with one fan saying: "Tears running down my face [sic] and then the blue penis."

Another posted a screenshot of the moment as they added a series of laughing emoji's whilst penning: "Prince Harry's blue penis."

The cheeky episode began with a disclaimer saying all characters - even if they're based on real people - are fictional.

But fans were quick to point out the prince’s red hair and his wife’s floppy hat drew similarities to the duke and duchess.
