John OHurley Biography

John O'Hurley is an American actor, voice actor, and television personality born on October 9, 1954, in Kittery, Maine, and raised in New England. O'Hurley is best known for his roles as J. Peterman on the television series "Seinfeld" and as the host of the game show "Family Feud" from 2006 to 2010.
