Full list of school closures as snow and ice warnings hit UK today - are you affected?

SCHOOLS up and down the country are closing classrooms today as Britain is hit by snow and ice warnings.

The Met Office has issued a number of yellow weather warnings for snow and ice across England, Wales, Scotland and Northern Ireland.

The mercury fell well below zero last night, and Met Office meteorologist Tom Morgan said the cold weather is set to hang around into next week.

He said: “This is a cold spell lasting around five to seven days so I think by the weekend if you’re living in Scotland or Northern Ireland, you will feel it getting a bit milder – but it will remain cold in England and Wales.

“It may well take until the early part of next week for temperatures to return to normal across England and Wales.”

Dozens of schools have already announced they will be closed today.

Oxford

Appleton Church of England Primary School will be closed due to no power and no heating, the earliest expected time that it will reopen is 10.30am.

There was a power cut across Appleton this morning, with 500 homes going without power since around 7.30am.
