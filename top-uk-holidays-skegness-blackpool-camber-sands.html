Top UK holidays: Read before you book your trip to Skegness, Blackpool and Camber Sands

IF you're planning a family holiday then don't overlook the glorious getaways right on your doorstep.

We've teamed up with hundreds of UK parks to give our readers bargain holidays from £9.50.

Here's our guide to the best picks in the much-loved areas of Blackpool, Skeggy and the Camber Sand seaside.

Skegness

With its golden sands and fun for all the family, Skegness is one of the UK’s favourite holiday resorts. It’s part of 50 miles of breathtaking Lincolnshire coastline, with a host of attractions easily reached by car or public transport. Here are some of the parks you can stay at in the area.

The Richmond Holiday Centre

Music, dance and fun are all part of your holiday at The Richmond and the spacious entertainment centre provides a variety of family entertainment including live cabaret, comedy, family's discos, bingo kids games and more.

For bird lovers and walkers, the park is situated just just three miles away from nature reserve Gibraltar Point and is only a 10-15 minute walk from the town centre.

Park Resorts Southview

The park is beautifully landscaped featuring a nine-hole golf course and two stocked fishing lakes. There’s an Indoor swimming pool, Jacuzzi, flume, sauna, gym and a family changing room. You can also pop into the Boathouse Bar &Restaurant or arcade and Showbar.

There is also a sparkling entertainment programme fun for the whole family. The children will have lots of fun on the playground, play park and multi sports court.

Plus, you can explore the local area including donkey rides on the beach and the lively seaside resort of Skegness only a short drive away.

Fantastic choice of caravan holiday homes including a choice to upgrade to models with double glazing and central heating

Butlin’s Skegness

A Butlin’s break is the perfect for family time together beside the sea. With so many facilities and entertainment indoor and out rain never stops play, making it the perfect family break whatever the weather.

Where else can you have a walk along the beach, visit the fairground, take a dip in Splash Waterworld and see a fantastic show all in one day?

Whether you are looking for character shows for tots, unique experiences for school age children or a programme full of talent for all ages, Butlin’s has something to make your whole family smile.

Golden Palm Resort

Situated at Chapel St Leonards near Skegness, the Golden Palm Resort is an all round family park with fun for all ages.

The centre of attention in the evenings is the magnificent Club Tropicana where there is always a varied programme of live entertainment. The fabulous Theatre Bar will mix your favourite cocktail or you can enjoy a tasty bar meal.

Children are well looked after with their own programme of activities in Goldie's Lounge Family Room along with our multi-age indoor play room.

The park also boasts outdoor play area's, multi use games area and a tennis court. The Golden Palm arcade has all the latest video games, our floodlit crazy golf is always a family favourite too. Our indoor fun pool offers great fun for all the family complete with mini splash pool and hair raising flumes.

Accommodation at Golden Palm Resort is in superb modern caravan homes, all with fitted kitchens, separate bedrooms, bathroom and colour TV.

Golden Anchor Caravan Park

Conveniently situated next to the sand dunes on Trunch Lane in Chapel St. Leonards, Golden Anchor is the sister park to our main resort, Golden Palm.

Golden Anchor has a unique location only seconds away from the sandy beach of Chapel St. Leonards. The park has a quiet and relaxing atmosphere, ideal for a peaceful family getaway.

You will find a vast array of unique shops, amusements, cafes and restaurants, along with a huge choice of family friendly bars and clubs offering fantastic live entertainment all within easy walking distance of the park.

Centrally located between the village centres of Chapel St. Leonards and Ingoldmells, Golden Anchor is an ideal base to explore the Lincolnshire coast.

Please note pets are not permitted at Golden Anchor Caravan Park.

Coastfields Holiday Village

Only four miles from Skegness, Coastfields Holiday Village is the perfect place to enjoy a traditional family caravan holiday.

The well equipped and modern holiday homes will meet your every requirement, with superb onsite facilities, including a heated indoor swimming pool with slide, supermarket, bars and arcades.

The whole family will be amazed by the live entertainment shows in the 500 seater showbar or you can simply relax in the licensed Aqua Lounge, also serving Starbucks coffee and tasty treats, and with free Wi-Fi available.

Blackpool

Take in the sights from the dizzy heights of the Blackpool Tower Eye and it’s breath-taking SkyWalk or discover the depths of the scary yet funny Blackpool Tower Dungeon featuring amazing special effects and incredible actor-led show.

Haven Marton Mere

Look up and you can spot Blackpool Tower from this park – the bright lights and action are a few miles away. But sit beside the Mere and you’d never know it as you have the best of both worlds here.

The pool and sports facilities are perfect for family get-togethers – or beating dad at adventure golf. Because this park is nice and flat it’s also easy for everyone to get around.

There is always something to see and do for all ages, from your toddler seeing sand for the very first time and learning to swim in one of our swimming pools to you trying your hand at archery or simply relaxing whilst you're entertained by one of the shows.

Camber Sands

If you are looking for a beach holiday then look no further than Camber Sands.

With miles of sand dunes and fabulous golden sands, one of the best beaches in Britain is on your doorstep.

Parkdean Resorts Camber Sands

You can spend the day making sandcastles and splashing in the sea while staying at Parkdean Resorts.

But if you prefer pool rather than ocean, the resort boasts four pools and a flume as well as a spa bath, sauna and solarium.

There's something for the kids too from crazy golf course to go karts, adventure playground and sports court.

After hard day’s work having fun and running round, you can put your feet up and watch the live evening family show.
