Photos Of The TV Star Hollywood Life

Sharon Osbourne has been a big star in Hollywood for over two decades. Here’s the best photos of the Sharon.
Sharon Osbourne has always been an open book in Hollywood. As a talk show host, she’s discussed topics like her husband Ozzy’s cheating scandal as well as her plastic surgery. As ‘The Talk’ kicked off its 10th season back in 2019, the mom-of-three revealed she had undergone her fourth facelift.

“It was five weeks ago today I had my operation and I had my neck done, my jowls gone, and they basically went ‘whoop’ like this,’ making a motion from her neck to the top of her head ‘but everything was just lifted up so it looks more refreshed and more tight.” In 2016 on the show she revealed, “Well, I’ve got my third face on right now. Face three and counting.’ She added about aging, ‘I think you get more beautiful on the inside and your aura is more beautiful, but definitely you get more wrinkles with that beauty.”
