Cole &amp; Dylan Sprouse Then &amp; Now: Photos Hollywood Life

Cole and Dylan Sprouse have grown up before our eyes and you can check out their best photos from over the years here.

The twins have gone from child stars to bonafide hunks in Hollywood. They started out in the sitcom ‘Grace Under Fire’ before getting their breakout role in the 1999 comedy ‘Big Daddy.’ A few years later, the twins would star in the hit Disney Channel series ‘The Suite Life of Zack & Cody.’ The show ran for 3 seasons, and then the boys got a spinoff called ‘The Suite Life on Deck.’

Following 3 seasons of that show and a movie, Cole and Dylan decided to take a break from acting. They went to New York University and graduated in 2015. Cole returned to acting in 2017 when he was cast as Jughead Jones on ‘Riverdale.’ Dylan has since returned to acting as well, notably starring as Trevor in ‘After We Collided.’ The duo has enjoyed so many adorable moments over the years.
