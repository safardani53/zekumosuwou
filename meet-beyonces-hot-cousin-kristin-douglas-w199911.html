Meet Beyonces Hot Cousin Kristin Douglas

Talk about good genes! Beyoncé posed for a picture with her genetically blessed cousin Kristin Douglas, which was posted on Instagram on Sunday, March 19. Previously unknown, Queen Bey’s gorgeous cuz has made quite a splash on social media.

The “Formation” singer, 34, attended the funeral of her uncle Skip Beyoncé over the weekend, where she caught up with Douglas (on the right) and another unidentified person.

“Celebrating the life of Uncle Skip! #ripuncleskip,” Beyonce’s stunning relative captioned the ladies’ reunion photo.

Upon taking a pic with the superstar, Douglas’ online popularity has dramatically increased. Now with 53,200 (and counting!) followers, fans pointed out that the self-described “nurse” and “mom to two beautiful children” only had a couple thousand less than a week ago.

“Not that I'm stalking or anything, but Beyonce's highly blessed cousin had 2,000 followers yesterday. Now?” one Twitter user wrote alongside a screenshot showing Douglas’ growing follower-count.

Of course, the internet has also taken notice of Douglas’ undeniable good looks.

“Would ride 100 floors in an elevator with Beyonce's sister for a chance with Beyonce's cousin,” a fan tweeted in reference to Solange Knowles attacking brother-in-law Jay Z during an elevator ride after the 2014 Met Ball. “Good lord.”

Added another, “Has black Twitter found Beyoncé's fine cousin yet?”

This isn’t the first time Beyoncé-related content has shown up on Douglas’ Instagram feed. More than two years ago, she posted a meme featuring the pop diva’s 4-year-old daughter, Blue Ivy; and 59 weeks ago, she expressed her indignation over nonstop Houston traffic by uploading a snapshot of an annoyed-looking Bey labeled, “I’m over it.”
