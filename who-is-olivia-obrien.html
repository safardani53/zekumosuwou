Who is Olivia O'Brien?

Olivia O'Brien was born on November 26, 1999, in Thousand Oaks, California.

Olivia is a singer and songwriter who began her professional career in 2013.

She is currently signed with the record label, Island Records.

She earned the contract with Island Records after she collaborated with Gnash on the single, I Hate U, I Love U, which peaked at number 10 on the Billboard Hot 100 chart in the United States and number one in Australia.
