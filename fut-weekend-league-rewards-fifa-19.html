When do FUT Weekend League Rewards for Fifa 19 come out?

FIFA 19 FUT Champions rewards the best players with the best prizes to help cement the football game as the biggest selling sports franchise in video game history.

It was estimated that 80% of EA Sports players play FUT meaning there's a huge demand for the highly-coveted rewards. Here's the lowdown on when they're released...

When do FUT Weekend League rewards for Fifa 19 come out?

Each FUT Weekend League will last from Friday until Sunday, after which time EA reviews the results in order to fairly distribute the rewards.

The weekly rewards are then released the following Thursday after the League closes, at 8 am UK time, or 3 am in the United States and Canada.

Prizes will be unlocked at the same time for Xbox One, PS4, and PC users but only accounts that have completed a minimum of five matches are eligible.

The best Weekend League players may also be invited to future EA competitive gaming events.

More on FIFA 19

How to Qualify for the FIFA 19 Weekend League?

To qualify for the FUT Weekend League, you’ll need Champions Points which you can earn by playing games as part of Division Rivals in Ultimate Team.

The better you play, the more points you'll earn, with a minimum of 2,000 needed to buy into Weekend League.

You also earn Champions Points at every rank in Weekend League, meaning players will need to achieve at least Silver 1 rank to earn enough points for re-qualification.

For FIFA 19, EA slightly tweaked the format so once you secure qualification for the league, you're able to defer your entry until a weekend that suits you.
