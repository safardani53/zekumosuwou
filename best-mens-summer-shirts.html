10 best men's summer shirts UK 2023, including Uniqlo and Boden

It might not seem like it, but summer is around the corner.

That means out with the knitwear, layering and overcoats and in with lightweight, breezy fabrics and (hopefully) short sleeves.

Investing in summer shirts is one of the easiest ways to brighten up your wardrobe and make the most of the warmer weather.

Whether you go for something made from linen, cotton, pique or tencel, summer shirts are essential for the morning commute or your poolside holiday.

From what to look out for, which styles to buy and the best men’s summer shirts around, you’re in the right place. 

What to look for when buying men's summer shirts

Fabric 

Summer shirts are all about the fabric, but which one you go for will drastically change the look.

There’s linen, of course. This lightweight, loosely woven fibre is extremely breathable and has a distinct texture, making it instantly recognisable.

There’s cotton as well, which is slightly denser but still soft against the skin and perfect for patterned or striped shirts.

Other fabrics like pique cotton are slightly more technical and designed to keep the wearer dry. Regardless of which you go for, just make sure the fabric is lightweight so it won’t weigh you down. 

Style 

As you can imagine, there are numerous shirt styles available for summer. Go for a regular point collar for something smarter, or aim for a button-down for a smart casual staple that can be dressed up or down.
