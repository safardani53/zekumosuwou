Jeff Ross Biography

American stand-up comedian and actor Jeff Ross is born on September 13, 1965, in Springfield, New Jersey, United States. Jeff has been keeping his personal matters to himself. There is no information related to his love affairs and married life. But it is believed that he is married and he has also been with his parents several times.

Jeff was enrolled at Jonathan Dayton High School. After that, he joined Boston University and majored in communication. He lost his father and mother at a young age. While he was in his college, he worked at BU's WTBU as the director, and later he joined the local NPR affiliate. In early 1990, he appeared on a Monday night open mic night. He played his first movie role in the 1996 movie, Celtic Pride. These days, he is serving as the Roastmaster General of the New York Friars' Club. As of 2022, his net worth is expected to be around $4 million.
