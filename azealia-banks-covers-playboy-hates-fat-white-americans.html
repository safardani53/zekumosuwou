Azealia Banks Covers Playboy, Hates &quot;Fat, White Americans&quot;

Usually, when a celebrity does a Playboy pictorial, most of the press coverage is about the photos themselves.

That’s not the case with Azealia Banks, whose most noteworthy body part will always be her mouth.

We first learned that Azealia would pose nude for Playboy last month, when the outspoken rapper broke the news on Twitter.

While the first photos have yet to be revealed, we get the feeling Banks’ comments in her interview will get more scrutiny than her photo spread (in which she reportedly bares all, BTW).

In her most extensive Q&A to date, Banks opens up about everything from her feud with Iggy Azalea to her controversial views on race:

On America: "I hate everything about this country. Like, I hate fat white Americans. All the people who are crunched into the middle of America, the real fat and meat of America, are these racist conservative white people who live on their farms."

On Race and Twitter: “It’s always about race. Lorde can run her mouth and talk sh-t about all these other bitches, but y’all aren’t saying she’s angry. If I have something to say, I get pushed into the corner..y’all mother–kers still owe me reparations! That’s why it’s still about race."

On Race and Music: "I get annoyed with the fact that I’m even asked to explain myself. Why do I have to explain this to y’all? My little white fans will be like, ‘Why do you want reparations for work you didn’t do?’

"Well, you got handed down your grandfather’s estate and you got to keep your grandmother’s diamonds and pearls and sh*t. I get upset when people are like, ‘Why don’t you just make music?’ What would happen if I couldn’t sing? Then I’d just be another black bitch to y’all."

Like we said, the woman has a lot to say.

The magazine hits stands Friday, and as far as what we can expect from the photospread, Banks recently described it thusly:

"I don’t think you see any, like, pink…I don’t know. I don’t mind nudity."

So yeah…it sounds like the pictorial will be every bit as revealing as the interview. We can’t wait!
