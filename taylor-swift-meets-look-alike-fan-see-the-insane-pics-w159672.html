It’s a face she knows “All Too Well”! Taylor Swift is known for her celebrity squad, but now her fan doppelgänger has come out of the woodwork as well, and the pictures are insane! On Friday, December 11, 19-year-old Brit Olivia Sturgiss met her celebrity look-alike Swift, 26, in Melbourne, Australia.

The epic moment was captured by Sturgiss’ pal Kristy Lee on her Instagram page, and fans immediately freaked out.

“Charlie’s Angels @taylorswift @taylornation #taylorswift #loft89 #noitsliv #taylorlookalike #taylorswiftlookalike #1989tourmelbourne,” Lee captioned the photo of the trio showing off their best Charlie’s Angels pose.

And while this is Sturgiss’ first time meeting Swift, it’s certainly not the first time she’s been compared to the Grammy-winning artist. With her blonde locks cut into the singer’s signature bob, her angular bone structure, petite frame, and affinity for crop tops and red lipstick, she’s basically Swift’s spitting image.

Her Instagram profile, @olivia_oblivious, has now amassed 11,500 followers, despite the fake account @oliviasturgiss stealing some of her thunder.

One side-by-side shot of the pair in matching poses sporting pale-pink sunglasses demonstrates the uncanny resemblance.

“Liv freaking me out. @taylornation we will be in section D4 on the 10th, section 7, Level 1 [east] Aisle 6, Row P on the 11th and section 79 row A on the 12th! Can’t wait to see you again! @taylorswift #taylorswift #taylornation,” Lee captioned the comparison photo.
