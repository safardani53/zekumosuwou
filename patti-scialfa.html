Patti Scialfa Biography

Patti Scialfa was very much passionate about music and was writing lyrics since her childhood. She professionally started her singing career as a back vocalist. She faced big hurdles along the way to fame and success in the music industry, there even was a time when she had to work as a waitress. However, the determined girl never gave up her dreams and finally made it big with her engagement in the band "cats on a smooth surface" which got her huge exposure. After that, she joined the E Street Band, and also performed for Rolling Stones' "Dirty Work" album, Keith Richard's Solo album is "Talk is Cheap".

She is a friend of the famous Soozie Tyrell and Lisa Lowell and has shared the stage with them as well. Patti has recorded 3 solo albums "Rumble Doll”, “Street Lullaby" and "Play It as Is Lays". One of the fascinating things about her is that she is married to Bruce Springsteen who is also an American by nationality, and belongs to Irish, Dutch and Italian ethnicity. Patti's husband is also one of the most famous people in the music industry. The couple was married in the year 1991 and they have 3 children in total. The Springsteen family is now living in Colts Neck, New Jersey. Patti Scialfa has a net worth of $50 million and also owns many houses scattered across the country.
