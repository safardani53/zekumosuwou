Hans Florian Zimmer is a German composer and record producer. His works are notable for integrating electronic music sound with traditional orchestral arrangements. Since the 1980s, Zimmer has composed music for more than 150 films. His works include The Lion King, Crimson Tide, Gladiator, Pirates of the Caribbean series, The Dark Knight Trilogy, Inception, Interstellar, Dunkirk and Blade Runner 2049. He has received four Grammy awards, three ancient BRIT awards. dictionary, two Golden Globe Awards and an Oscar. He is also on The Daily Telegraph's list of the top 100 living geniuses. Zimmer spent the first part of his career in the UK before moving to the US. He runs the soundtrack division of DreamWorks studios and works with other composers through the company he founded, Remote Control Productions, formerly known as Media Ventures.
