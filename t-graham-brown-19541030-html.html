Anthony Graham Brown, known professionally as T. Graham Brown, is an American country music singer. Active since 1973, Brown has recorded a total of 13 studio albums and has more than 20 singles on the Billboard Hot Country Songs chart. Three of those singles - “Hell and High Water” and “Don't Go to Strangers” from 1986, and “Darlene” from 1988 - reached number one, and eight more reached the Top Ten.
