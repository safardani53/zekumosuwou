5 Best Puff Sleeve Dresses 2021 | The Sun UK

THE statement sleeve is back for another season, so we've picked out the best puff sleeve dresses here to get in on the trend this summer.

Puff sleeves are a silhouette that have been doing the rounds for a while now; whether seen on romantic peasant dresses, eighties-esque cocktail frocks or elegant floral maxis.

It's a style that can be super-wearable and comfy too. Think statement shapes that skim over your arms for the ultimate in summer breeziness, and voluminous proportions that are capable of drawing the eye in and balancing your frame.

Whatever your style, there's a plethora of puff sleeve dresses available to shop now, whether you're looking for something laid-back and casual or worthy of an Instagram moment. It's up to you how 'puffy' you go too: there are just as many subtle designs as there are super-sized shoulders, so find what speaks to you most.

One thing we also love about the best puff sleeve dresses are their ability to completely elevate the rest of your outfit; be it drawing attention to your necklace, contrasting with a pair of plimsolls or allowing the rest of your accessories to take a back seat.

Take a look below at our pick of the best puff sleeve dresses to amp up your wardrobe this season.
