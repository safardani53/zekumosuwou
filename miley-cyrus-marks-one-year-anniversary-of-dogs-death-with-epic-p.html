Miley Cyrus Marks One-Year Anniversary of Dog's Death With Epic Pooch Party

One year ago today, Miley Cyrus’ dog, Floyd, died in horrific fashion.

It’s always hard to lose a beloved pet, but it was particularly difficult in Miley’s case, as Floyd was killed by coyotes, and sources close to the singer say that she still blames herself for his death.

For weeks after Floyd’s passing, Miley would break down on stage while performing, and friends grew concerned as she sank into a deep depression.

12 months later, Miley is clearly still feeling the pain, but she’s decided to mark the anniversary of Floyd’s death in classic Miley fashion. 

That’s right, she threw a big, gaudy party and invited all over her closest friends and their pups. 

"Tomorrow it will have been 1 year since Floyd passed away," Miley wrote, in a message to her fans on Instagram. "At times I didn’t know how I was going to go out there and perform and smile and pretend I wasn’t hurting.

"But with you I didn’t have to. I could show my pain and you all would console me. I love you."

Gratitude to her fans and a brightly-colored, festive memorial. Maybe Miley could teach us all a lesson on how to grieve productively.

We just hope none of those pooches went home with contact highs.
