Stephen Bear is refused entry to bar after he turns up 'begging to speak to ex Charlotte Crosby' as

STEPHEN Bear was refused entry from a top bar and restaurant after turning up without a reservation in a desperate bid to speak to ex-girlfriend Charlotte Crosby.

The reality star looked less than impressed as he argued with security at Sheesh in Chigwell before giving up and walking away.

The Ex On The Beach star ended up getting turned away because he hadn’t made a reservation for their hugely popular Sheesh Roc Wednesdays.

He was also wearing trainers - which is strictly against the venue’s entry policy.

A source said: “Bear showed up and wanted to speak to Charlotte who was inside. He hadn’t booked and he was wearing completely the wrong clothes, so there was no way he was getting in.

“He was making a scene claiming he wasn’t allowed in because of Charlotte - despite the fact that Sheesh is very clear about its entry requirements.

“At one point he tried to get Marnie to get Charlotte for him, but things got a little heated and even Pete ended up getting involved.

“He eventually got the message and left, so Charlotte could enjoy her evening in peace.”

It certainly didn’t seem to worry Charlotte, 27, who couldn’t have looked happier as she danced with Pete - unconcerned about who saw them.

An insider told us: “Charlotte was trying it on with Pete the whole night. She was being pretty obvious about it and didn’t seem to mind who saw.

“Everyone saw them together - they were all over each other, clearly enjoying each other’s company.”

The source added: “They are both single, so why not have some fun?”

Their evening out comes just weeks after romance rumours were first sparked when the pair looked cosy on a night out with friends.
