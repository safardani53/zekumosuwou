Is Incubus' frontman Brandon Boyd dating 'Starz' actress Sarah Hay?

Often seen paired with beautiful women, the ever-so-mystic and charismatic Brandon Boyd has recently been rumored to be dating Sarah Hay.

So, is Hay his new girlfriend? Is the musician back in the dating scene after ending a 10-year-old relationship with Baelyn Elspeth?

Boyd and Hay's Instagram Affair

On February 14, 2020, the charismatic singer posted a painting by him, entitled “Sarah with red spirals.” The painting was of Hay's nude art with a red spiral drawn in some parts of her body.

In his caption, he went on to thank Hay for allowing him to capture her.

But the answer to whether the two are dating or 'buddies' who are working together is still a mystery. But with recent Instagram posts we lean towards the latter.

Brandon Boyd’s Past Dating Life

After entering the music scene, the singer has been paired with numerous beautiful models. His fans are often curious about which of his girlfriends would end up being his wife, but he still hasn't taken things past the dating realm.

Out of all his lovers, he had a long run with Carolyn Murphy, an Estee Lauder supermodel, and his most recent long-term girlfriend — Baelyn Elspeth.

Elspeth and Boyd were together for a decade. During an interview with Sharp magazine, the singer revealed that his album 8 has bits and pieces of his ex-girlfriend.

Besides, he also mentioned how he wrote the album while he was in the process of unfolding his relationship with his model girlfriend.

...it [his relationship with Elspeth] was like we folded in together and we were this lovely piece of origami for 10 years and then we unfolded. And now we’re still very much best friends. She’ll have some great insight as to the psychologies of some of these songs.

Brandon Boyd Was Chased By Girls In The Third Grade

The lead singer of Incubus can easily be portrayed as an icon for many. His infamous shirtless videos are to be blamed, but sources claim that he has been a chick-magnet since his early childhood days.

In an exclusive interview with Angel Fire, Boyd’s mother, Dolly Wiseman, and brother, Darren, came forward and shared some private details about his life.

They collectively shared that Boyd has always had a charismatic effect on girls, but he was usually shy around them. 

Even when he was a small child, he had this charisma… Girls loved him. He had pale blond hair and big brown eyes, and even his teachers wanted to touch his hair.

The singer's brother shared an incident from when the singer was in third grade. Darren recalled how an older girl would kiss and maul Boyd when their mother came to pick them up from school. But him being a shy kid, he would cry and push her off. 

Similarly, his mother shared another incident when he was in fourth grade, and a sixth-grade girl followed him home. He invited the girl over to play. Later, the singer went on to the kitchen and told his mother that the girl had been kissing him and he liked it.
