Adidas x South Park Towelie trainers - here's when they go on sale

ADIDAS and South Park have partnered together for the launch of a very special pair of trainers.

To celebrate the unofficial holiday of 4/20, they've designed a pair of kicks inspired by legendary character Towelie.

Fans of the show will be aware with Towelie's antics, and is well known for consistently getting high throughout the series, making him the perfect choice for the limited edition pair.

The Adidas Towelie trainers are a Campus 80' pair that have been given a new lease of life.

Sole Collector has reported that the Campus 80's have been wrapped in a purple terrycloth material; just like a towel, with Towelie's eyes on the sneaker's tongue.

There's even a hidden pocket under the sneaker's tongue.

Plus, the eyes are UV sensitive and will turn bloodshot and red when you've been in the sun - you've guessed, just like Towelie's comical high appearance in South Park.

An usual collaboration, it's sure to be a hit with South Park fans, with many already saying they can't wait to get their hands on a pair, with many saying "need these", and one saying "Someone just take my money now".

But when can they go on sale? Well you guessed it, they'll launch on 4/20; that's the 20 April, and will be available from adidas.co.uk.

The trainers are expected to be be a part of a wider South Park collection, so you'll have to wait and see what other South Park favourites will be included in the collection.

Set your alarms for April 20, they're available from adidas.co.uk.
