Who is Shaun Clarida:

Shaun Clarida is a famous Bodybuilder. He was born on September 5, 1982 and his birthplace is United States. Often referred to as The Giant Killer, he is a multiple IFBB Olympian who is a member of the Jacked Factory Team.
