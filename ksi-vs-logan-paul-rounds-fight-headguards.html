KSI vs Logan Paul: How many rounds is the fight, how long are the rounds and will the pair be wearin

YOUTUBE superstars KSI and Logan Paul go head-to-head in the boxing ring tonight.

The highly anticipated showdown between the two online sensations will take place at the Manchester Arena.

KSI vs Logan Paul fight: Live stream, what TV channel, start time, ticket info and full undercard info for YouTube boxing match

How many rounds is the fight between KSI and Logan Paul?

The showdown will take place over six rounds.

But Paul will be aware that it only took KSI three rounds to beat Joe Weller in his previous fight.

Midway through the third round, Weller was given a standing count and then the referee was forced to stop the fight shortly after the restart.

YouTube stars KSI and Logan Paul weigh-in and face off before huge fight at Manchester Arena

Will KSI and Logan Paul be wearing headguards?

KSI wore a headguard during his last fight with Weller.

But Paul has previously requested that the pair do not wear head guards during this fight.

When is the fight between KSI and Logan Paul?

The pair will fight on Saturday, August 25.

It will be held at the Manchester Arena.

The event will start at 7pm.

Which channel is it on and can I live stream it?

You will be able to watch YouTube - not surprising seeing as they are both stars on the online channel.

The fight will, however, set you back £7.50 through YouTube.

Alternatively, you will be able to keep up-to-date with all the latest action from Manchester on the SunSport LIVE blog.
