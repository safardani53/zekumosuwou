Suits Season 3 Preview: Louis Litt Melts Down Over Office Supplies

Pearson Hardman’s power-hungry senior partner Louis Litt isn’t happy with the way he’s being treated at his company in the wake of a merger with a top British firm — and he wants everyone to know it! When Suits‘ third season premieres Tuesday, July 16 on USA, Louis (Rick Hoffman) is fuming over everything from the merger to a lack of office supplies.

“God dammit!” Louis wails to Harvey Spector’s (Gabriel Macht) legal secretary Donna (Sarah Rafferty) in the emotional preview clip above. “I’ll tell you what happened: This stupid merger!” Ranting about how he feels his position in the company is being devalued, Louis later goes on a tirade about his favorite pens being replaced by a more cost-effective brand.

“The new quartermaster will not buy us a single Uniball in this entire firm. We’re stuck with these stupid plastic [pens] that keep breaking,” he yelps.
