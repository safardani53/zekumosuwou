New iPhone 11 deal for 29 a month with 9GB of data saves you 127

A CRACKING deal on Apple's latest smartphone will save you at least £127 – and possibly even more.

The new iPhone 11 has only been out a month, but it's already being sold at bargain prices online.

One such offers is available at Mobiles.co.uk, which is flogging the new handset for £29 a month.

You'll need to pay a £290 upfront fee, but that only brings the total price to £986.

It gets you unlimited minutes and texts, plus 9GB of data to use each month with EE.

That's a little bit higher than the £729 retail price for the iPhone 11. But when you factor in the cost of buying a SIM card, the savings start to add up.

  • iPhone 11 at EE (9GB data) for £29 a month (£290 upfront) – buy now

EE doesn't offer a 9GB SIM only plan, but there are some similar options.

The nearest you can get is the 20GB plan for £20 a month.

That works out at £480 over two years. Add that on to the price of your phone, and you get £1,209.

So that's an impressive saving of £223 with this deal – although you'll have less data.

The only cheaper SIM offer is 1GB of data for £16 a month.

That costs £384 over two years, bringing the total cost of the phone and SIM to £1,113.

So you're still saving £127 even with EE's cheapest SIM deal.

Plus you'll get an additional 8GB of data per month, making the deal even better.
