Photos of the Saturday Night Live Alum Hollywood Life

Pete Davidson made history in 2014 when he joined Saturday Night Live as the youngest cast member in its history when he was just 21 years old and you can see photos of him in honor of his birthday, here.

Since then, Pete Davidson has become a major fan-favorite for his hilarious sketches and Weekend Update segments. News he was leaving the show emerged on May 2022, meaning the end of an eight-season era.

He’s become almost as famous for his dating life, too. He had a whirlwind relationship with Ariana Grande in 2018, to whom he got engaged after just a few weeks of dating. They split that fall after their short-lived engagement. After that, he dated Kate Beckinsale, Margaret Qualley, Kaia Gerber, and Phoebe Denovor. Then, he really got people talking after going public with the one and only Kim Kardashian. After that? Rumors of a romance with model Emily Ratajkowski began.

Pete has been very candid about his health issues as well. Over the years, he’s opened up to his fans about Crohn’s Disease, borderline personality disorder, bipolar disorder, and addiction.
