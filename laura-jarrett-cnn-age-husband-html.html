Laura Jarrett Age 33, CNN Reporter &amp; Husband Wedding That Had VVIP Guests

American TV reporter Laura Jarrett fulfilled her dream of marrying her soulmate when she tied the knots with her college sweetheart, Tony Balkissoon. On her wedding day, her happiness had no limits when the former President of USA "Barack Obama" and his entire family attended the wedding!

The Chicago native is an American litigation attorney who works as a political reporter for CNN mostly based in Washington, DC. She is the daughter of famous Valerie June Jarrett, former government official and senior advisor to former US President, Barack Obama.

Born in the family of politicians and reporters, she ended up following the footsteps of her parents to become a reporter herself.

Laura Jarrett's Bio: Parents Divorced When She Was Just 5 Years Of Age

The CNN diva was born in Chicago, Illinois in 1983 to well-to-do parents, Valerie Jarrett, former government official, and William Jarrett, a doctor.

She was born in a wealthy and well-educated family. Sadly, Laura's parents divorced in 1988 when she was just 5. At the age of 10, another tragedy struck her life, as she mourned the loss of her father, who died from a heart attack, aged 40. 

As a child, the CNN reporter attended various schools in Chicago. A bright student, she had a knack for more intellectual subject matters even during her early age. After completing high school, the attorney, aged 33, she joined Harvard Law School.

While studying in Harvard Law School, she portrayed roles as Articles Selection co-chair, a technical editor as well as an Article Editor, for the Harvard Journal; which covered Law and Gender.

Adding to that, she even published her articles on the intersection of gender, violence, and the law. The Chicago native then graduated with a degree in Law in 2010.

After graduations, Laura went on to practice law in both federal and state court in Illinois.

Laura Jarrett’s CNN Career, Her Net Worth 

Before practicing Law, Jarrett worked as a judicial Law clerk for Rebecca Pallmeyer, honorable judge of United States District Court of Illinois and later for Honorable Ann Williams on Seventh Circuit Court of Appeals.

Prior to that, she worked as a litigation attorney in her hometown of Chicago. She prioritized on the defense of companies and individuals as well as some pro bono cases such as sex trafficking.

The Chicago native joined CNN as a reporter in September 2016 based in Washington, D.C. bureau.

Laura Jarrett’s High Profile Wedding With Husband; President Obama As Guest

Jarrett fulfilled her dream when she got married in 2012 to her long-time boyfriend and college sweetheart Tony Balkissoon. Her husband is the son of former Legislative Assembly of Ontario member Bas Balkissoon. 

The duo first crossed their paths during their college days in Harvard Law School. And after regularly seeing each other during their college days, they began to feel more connected to each other--so they started dating.

And after several dates, the duo, with a decision to fortify their relationship and stay committed to each other, got engaged at the Paris Club.
