This 25 Accessorize bag is just like a 795 Gucci one... but can you tell which is which?

ANOTHER day, another beautiful designer handbag we're lusting over.

And this time it's all about the monochrome Gucci chain bag that retails at a hefty £795... but luckily for our bank balances, an incredible Accessorize dupe is making us do a serious style double take.

  • Gucci Marmont monochrome mini bag, £795 from Farfetch - buy now

The perfect everyday size to throw over your shoulder (or across your body), this monochrome offering is a super chic addition to any outfit.

No matter how smart or casual.

After all, there's a reason the Gucci Marmont bags are a fashion blogger favourite.

But at just shy of £800, this is one beautiful bag that is going to remain firmly in our fashion dreams.

  • Quilted cross body bag, £25 from Accessorize - buy now

But thankfully Accessorize has come to the rescue of our purse strings again with this amazing designer dupe.

Seriously, even the most swotted-up style experts would struggle to tell the difference between the two.

Featuring an incredibly similar white and black print and a edgy gold chain to match, the only thing missing from this Accessorize bag is the tell-tale Gucci logo.

But we prefer it a bit more understated, in all honesty.

And with a £770 saving, you really can't go far wrong.
