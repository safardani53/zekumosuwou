Who is Megan Nicholls?

Megan, the daughter of champion National Hunt trainer Paul Nicholls, has always had an affiliation with horse racing.

She is a former jockey who rode Knappers Hill to victory in the Grade Two bumper at Aintree in 2021.

After a massive 110 career wins, Nicholls decided to retire at the age of 25 to focus on her media work.

She's now a horse racing pundit for ITV Racing and Racing TV.

Who is Megan Nicholls' dad Paul?

Paul Nicholls is an eleven-time winner of the National Hunt Trainers' Championship.

He has had over 3,000 winners during his time being a horse trainer, including 45 winners at Cheltenham Festival.

Nicholls has even had a Grand National winning horse in Neptune Collonges back in 2012.
