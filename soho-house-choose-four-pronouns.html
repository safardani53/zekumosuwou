Celeb club Soho House is giving members the option to choose up to FOUR pronouns

CELEBRITY haunt Soho House gives exclusive members the chance to choose up to FOUR pronouns.

The club chain, whose guests have included Leonardo DiCaprio, Margot Robbie, Maya Jama, and Prince Harry and Meghan, has the feature on its app and lets people pick the four from 41 choices when setting up their profile.

As well as “he”, “she” and non- binary “they”, it offers non-binary neopronouns, a word created for a pronoun without expressing gender, such as “ze” and “zir” or “co” and “cos”.

One member said: “I hadn’t heard of a lot of the options.

"I thought some might be a different language.”

The chain also asks members if they identify as LGBTQIA2S+, standing for lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, queer and/or questioning, intersex, asexual, two-spirit and other.

Two-spirit is a term for indigenous North Americans who show masculine and feminine traits.

A spokeswoman said: “Soho House has always been an inclusive space for our global members.”
