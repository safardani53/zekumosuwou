What is rapper Pooh Shiesty's age, net worth, and real name?

POOH Shiesty is a "gangster rapper" from Memphis, Tennesee, who has blown up online for his music.

On June 8, it was reported that Shiesty was arrested after he surrendered himself to Miami authorities.

What is rapper Pooh Shiesty's age, net worth, and real name?

Pooh, born Lontrell Williams, is signed to Gucci Mane's record label 1017.

The Memphis native, who is just 20-years-old, has been making ripples through the rap world since his debut.

His track "Back in Blood" featuring Lil Durk has peaked at No. 46 on the Billboard Hot 100 chart.

He dropped his mixtape Sheisty Season in February 2021.

The young rapper has amassed 1.6million followers on Instagram and draws in around 2million monthly listening streams on Spotify.

As of 2021, he is estimated to be worth around $1.5million reports ExactNetWorth.

Why was Pooh Shiesty arrested?

On June 8, DJ Akademiks tweeted that Shiesty had been arrested by Miami Police.

He confirmed that he had been charged with "aggravated assault/battery" for allegedly "discharging a firearm in a club where at least one person was hit with a bullet."

The Gucci Mane signee handed himself in to cops on to reportedly face charges for a shooting in a Miami-Dade strip club over Memorial Day weekend.

The incident happened at the King of Diamonds strip club in Northwest Miami-Dade on May 30, the Miami Herald reports.
