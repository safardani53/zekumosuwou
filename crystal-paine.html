Who is Crystal Paine:

Crystal Paine is a famous Blogger. She was born on October 1, 1981 and her birthplace is Kansas. Crystal is also well known as, Bargain-shopper and author who rose to fame for her personal finance blog, MoneySavingMom.com. She has authored the New York Times best-selling book, Money Making Mom as well as Say Goodbye to Survival Mode and A Thankful Heart is a Happy Heart. She later designed an e-course on the subject of Supermarket Savings.

Crystal is originated from United States. She and Dave Ramsey are both financial experts and New York Times best-selling authors who focus on helping people live debt-free.
