A DOG is lucky to be alive after falling off a 150ft cliff on to the beach below — and suffering no major injuries.

Owner Sandra Costin had been walking Button off-lead when the pup plunged over the edge of the rockface.

Coastguards were called out, spotted the dog and abseiled down to the sands below to grab her.

Button was placed in a rescue bag and winched to the top of the cliff close to Thornwick Bay Holiday Park in Bridlington, East Yorks.

Chuffed Sandra, a former NHS nurse, said: “She’s shocked and dazed and now resting at home.

“I was very stupid walking her without the lead on but she loves to run around.
