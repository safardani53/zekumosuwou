Who is Chris Stapleton's wife Morgane?

MORGANE Stapleton has had a successful career.

The singer-songwriter is a talented musician and a caring mother.

Who is Chris Stapleton's wife Morgane?

Morgane was born on January 25, 1983, in Nashville, Tennessee.

The 40-year-old provides background vocals for her husband, country singer Chris Stapleton. She also harmonizes and duets with him.

She plays the tambourine and runs a jewelry business called Tennessee Turquoise Company, whose store is located in Leiper's Fork in Franklin, Tennessee.

The female-run company is passionate about "Native American Culture & Community" and states that "every purchase supports the National Indigenous Women's Resource Center."

Morgane is active on social media and has 234K followers on Instagram, where she often shares photos featuring her husband and the two of them performing together.

On October 25, 2022, she posted a photo to her Instagram page of her and Chris performing at the Smoothie King Center in New Orleans, Louisiana on October 22.

She credited photographer Andy Barron.

Do Chris and Morgane have children?

Chris and Morgane have five children together, including four sons and one daughter.

Their eldest child is their 13-year-old son named Waylon, who was born in 2009.

The pair then welcomed their daughter Ada, 12, in November 2010.

In April 2018, Morgane gave birth to twin boys Macon and Samuel.

The couple's youngest child, a son, was born in 2019. Chris and Morgane have not yet revealed his name to the public.

How long has Chris Stapleton been married to his wife?

Chris and Morgane have been married for 15 years.

The pair tied the knot on October 27, 2007.

As PEOPLE noted, the couple met in 2003 while they were employed as songwriters at close-by music publishing businesses.

On October 27, 2022, Morgane took to Instagram to share a wedding anniversary post featuring her and her husband.

She wrote: "You're a keeper. Happy 15th, babe!"
