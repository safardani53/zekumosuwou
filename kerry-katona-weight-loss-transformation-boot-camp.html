Kerry Katona shows off new curves after admitting she's happy with weight gain before heading to boo

KERRY Katona shows off her figure on holiday in Greece while on an intense fitness retreat.

The former Atomic Kitten singer slipped into a black bikini as she played snooker on a GI Jane Bootcamp in Mykonos.

The star was pictured with a cue in hand in the sunny resort, while showing off her physique.

The 38-year-old posed beside the games table on vacation giving onlookers a closer look at her toned legs and torso.

She paused the game for a brief moment to re-hydrate in the heat.

Kerry - who has daughters Lilly, Molly, Heidi and son Maxwell - continued to put her sporting skills to the test as she was pictured leaning over to pot a snooker ball, showing off her posterior.

The mum-of-four has insisted she is "happy" with her appearance despite putting on some weight recently.

She recently shared a picture of her pulling at her stomach, while on the Greek island, wearing a leopard print bikini.

She captioned the Instagram post: "So some pics have popped up online of me!!! Yes I have put some weight on but am happy!! I’m proud of my body!! I might not look sexy but I felt it!!

"The main thing is my head is in the best place it’s been in years and that’s what matters!!"

Kerry - who has admitted to being a yo yo dieter over the years - has admitted she has had "ups and downs", but decided to go on the fitness retreat to keep her healthy lifestyle on track and maintain a positive state of mind.

She continued: "I’m at bootcamp @g.i.jane_bootcamp to give me a kick up the backside and to keep on track of my state of mind!!

"We all have ups and downs it’s how we deal with those downs that show our strength!!!"
