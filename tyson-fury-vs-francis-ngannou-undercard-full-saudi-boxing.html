Tyson Fury vs Francis Ngannou undercard: Who else is on huge Saudi boxing bill?

TYSON FURY makes his highly-anticipated return to the ring on Saturday against UFC icon Francis Ngannou - and the undercard is just as good.

Boxing fans haven't seen the Gypsy King in action since his April 2022 sixth-round TKO on Dillian Whyte.

But after months of x-rated rants, the WBC champion has negotiated a fight with MMA star Ngannou.

However, the Saudi Saturday night main event isn't the only thing to look forward to as there are some big names on the undercard.

And SunSport has all the information on who else will be fighting on the night.

Who is on the Tyson Fury vs Francis Ngannou undercard?

Before the huge fight at the top of the bill, fans will get the chance to see Fabio Wardley put his title on the line against David Adeleye.

Read more on Fury vs Ngannou

New Zealand icon Joseph Parker is all set to finally settle his bitter feud with Simon Kean.

And boxing veteran Carlos Takam will be in action against Ilunga Makubu's younger brother, Martin Bakole.

Full card below (subject to change):

  • Tyson Fury vs. Francis Ngannou
  • Fabio Wardley vs. David Adeleye
  • Joseph Parker vs. Simon Kean
  • Arslanbek Makhmudov vs. Junior Anthony Wright
  • Moses Itauma vs. Istvan Bernath
  • Carlos Takam vs. Martin Bakole
  • Jack McGann vs. Alcibiade Duran Galvan

When is Tyson Fury vs Francis Ngannou?

  • Tyson Fury's mouthwatering fight against Francis Ngannou will take place THIS Saturday, October 28.
  • The bout will take place in Riyadh, Saudi Arabia.
  • Expect ring-walks to get going from around 10pm BST / 5pm ET.
  • It will be a 10-round professional fight but Fury's WBC belt will NOT be on the line.
  • Exhibition rules will NOT be in place.
