Drake Cheated On Rihanna?

Drake flew out Dominique from Houston, Texas to attend his concert, MediaTakeOut reported. However, that’s not all she got from him — he allegedly paid for her plane ticket, hotel room and “VIP treatment,” as well as hooked up with her after the show.

We know that she was there, since she shared a video of him performing on Instagram (see below), captioning it, “Drizzy concert was turnt last night in Frankfurt, Germany.”

Rihanna & Drake’s Romantic Date — Will She Care?

If the report does turn out to be true, RiRi’s not letting it get to her — she headed out to dinner with Drake in Amsterdam on March 7, and they definitely are getting closer! Plus, we know that he’s her fantasy.

“I mean, I think about it. Like, ‘Man that would be good.’ We have fun together,” Drake recently admitted to Rolling Stone, “She’s cool and s–t.  But we’re just friends. That’s my dog for life.”

Plus, Rihanna and Drake are happy with where they are.

“The only commitment they want is to be able to keep what they have and not ruin a good thing,” a source close to the singers told HollywoodLife.com exclusively. “Neither of them are in a rush for anything. They are so chill and love what they’ve got.”
