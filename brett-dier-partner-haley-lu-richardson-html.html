Inside Brett Dier and Partner Haley Lu Richardsons Wedding Plans

Wedding bells are ringing for Brett Dier and his partner Haley Lu Richardson.

The two met a decade ago when they were shooting for the official spin-off of Pretty Little Liars, Ravenswood. Dier played the role of Luke Matheson, whereas Richardson portrayed Tess Hamilton in the supernatural drama.

The couple instantly hit it off while shooting Ravenswood in 2012. Soon, they were smitten with each other and started dating.

Initially, they kept their relationship under wraps and did not reveal it to the public. But soon, they caved in and announced that they were dating.

They started talking about each other in interviews and showed off their relationship on social media. Still, they spoke very little about their love life, valuing their privacy over people's interests.

While talking with Cosmopolitan in 2018, Richardson spoke about her partner. She mentioned how the two prioritized and enjoyed spending time with each other.

Similarly, she talked about how she was the one who proposed to Dier.

Haley Lu Richardson Proposed to Her Partner

Richardson dubbed her engagement day as the "best day ever." Everything was spontaneous since the Five Feet Apart actress did not know she would propose to her partner.

She said it was a "totally in the moment" thing, and she followed her gut feeling. They were at an outside strip mall at the moment, eating pizza.

All of a sudden, Richardson wanted to ask Dier to marry her, and she did so. Fortunately, the actor said "yes," and the pair shared an intimate moment at the strip mall.

Brett Dier and his partner Haley Lu Richardson posing for a picture. (Source: Instagram)

They were crying and holding each other, saying, "we're going to get married, we're going to be together forever." But unfortunately, they did not have an engagement ring, so they made one themselves.

They went over to a bush and made rings out of twigs. Then, they each went on one knee and proposed with those twigs.

Richardson later made an engagement ring "engraved like a twig."

Brett Dier's Wedding Plans

It is unknown if the pair are still together. The last post they had of each other on their Instagram was in November 2020.

Since then, they have not made public appearances both live and on social media. But, regardless of it all, the two had elaborate plans to get married.

Dier had planned her wedding with her partner and often mentioned it in interviews. For instance, he talked about it in detail in an interview with ET Canada in May 2019.

He first showed off his engagement ring, which was a tricolored one. It was made of one part gold, one part meteor, and one part dinosaur bone, with the insides lined by whiskey barrel wood.

He then revealed his wedding plans by saying that the couple wanted to marry in Sedona, Arizona. The reason behind it was that it looked like Mars, and they both thought they were from outer space.

However, it is unknown if Dier and his partner are married yet.
