Who is Robin Arzon:

Robin Arzon is a famous Non-Fiction Author. She was born on September 20, 1982 and her birthplace is Philadelphia, PA. Robin is also well known as, New York Times Best Selling Author who is predominantly recognized for the 2016 book Shut Up and Run: How to Get Up, Lace Up, and Sweat with Swagger. She is both a Road Runners Club of America-certified coach and a certified personal trainer.

Robin is originated from United States. In July 2017, she was photographed with comedian Kevin Hart.
