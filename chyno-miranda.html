Who is Chyno Miranda:

Chyno Miranda is a famous Pop Singer. He was born on November 15, 1984 and his birthplace is Caracas, Venezuela. Chyno is also well known as, Venezuelan pop singer known as Chino who forms half of the pop duo of Chino & Nacho alongside Miguel Ignacio Mendoza. He received a Latin Grammy Award for a 2010 Chino & Nacho album entitled Mi Nina Bonita. He has also won multiple Latin Billboard and Premios Lo Nuestro awards and he released the single “Tu Me Quemas” in 2014.

Chyno is originated from Venezuela. Chino & Nacho’s Mi Nina Bonita edged out Daddy Yankee’s album Daddy Yankee Mundial to earn the Grammy for Best Urban Music Album.
