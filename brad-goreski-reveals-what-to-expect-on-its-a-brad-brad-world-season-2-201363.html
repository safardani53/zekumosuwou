Brad Goreski Reveals What to Expect on It's a Brad Brad World Season 2!

Brad Goreski has come a long way in a short time. The celebrity stylist, who once stored designer dresses in his garage after parting ways with former employer Rachel Zoe, shows considerable progress when Bravo’s It’s a Brad Brad World returns with new episodes Wednesday, March 6.

The 35-year-old stopped by Us Weekly‘s New York City offices on Tuesday, March 5, to reveal what’s in store for the reality show’s second season.

“This season I have two new assistants,” he said of Thomas Phillips and Hannah Soboroff, who join original employee Lindsay Myers. “Business is really good. What people will get to see this season as opposed to last season is a lot of me working hands-on with my clients, including Rashida Jones, Minka Kelly, Stacy Keibler and Coco Rocha.”

Kelly, 32, appears in the first episode when Goreski styles her for the Jenny Packham fashion show; Jones pops up later in the season when the stylist readies her for the premiere of Celeste and Jesse Forever. “All of them have very big events to go to,” Goreski explained, “so you’ll see me working with them for red carpet events [and] photo shoots.”

As he continually proves himself in the fashion industry, Goreski said most of his famous clients “have come from recommendations from other people.”

During his chat with Us, Goreski also hinted that he’ll be expanding his empire. “Styling will always be the center of my business and it will always be my primary focus . . . but there is a bigger plan,” he said. “I do hope to increase what I do into a Brad Goreski brand. Do I know what that is at this point? Not right now. But there is definitely a focus on figuring out what the next logical step is for me.”

The second season of It’s a Brad Brad World premieres Wednesday, March 6, at 10 p.m. EST on Bravo.
