HEIDI'S CALL

Before the announced break, Heidi was put under fire for her judging of an unusual performance.

The audience went wild as one of the performers started dancing energetically, and even Terry sang along.

However, Heidi pressed her buzzer before the act had finished, followed by Simon as they wrapped up.

During judging, the model claimed that the act "wasn't enough" for her.

After the duo had left the stage, Sofia doubled down on her support for their performance, but Heidi snarked: "My 13-year-old daughter can play a little piano for you."
