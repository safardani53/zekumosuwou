Kyle Richards and Mauricio Umanskys Marriage Stronger Because of RHOBH

Stronger than ever! Kyle Richards and Mauricio Umansky’s love has spanned over two decades and a whole lot of The Real Housewives of Beverly Hills drama, and their marriage has remained rock solid through it all.

“[The show] has definitely has made them stronger 100 percent and they are just the cutest honestly,” Richards’ eldest daughter, Farrah Aldjufrie, tells Us Weekly exclusively on the new episode of the “Getting Real with the Housewives” podcast. “They have set such a great example for couples, married couples, people in relationships because they truly are a team.”

The couple share daughters Alexia, 24, Sophia, 20, and Portia, 12. Kyle shares Farrah, 31, with her ex-husband Guraish Aldjufrie.

“Through the show, I think it just makes them appreciate their time together even more because they’re both so busy and he’s running a whole company and is one of the top agents in the country, and she has so many obligations,” the real estate agent tells Us.

 She continued: “They try to spend a lot of time together and they really appreciate their time together. I definitely think it’s made them stronger.”

Farrah works alongside Umansky as a real estate agent at The Agency, but wouldn’t rule out becoming a full-time housewife in the future.
