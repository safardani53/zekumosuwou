Jennifer Audrey Coolidge is an American actress, comedian and activist. She is known for her roles as Jeanine Stifler in the American Pie series, Paulette Bonafonté Parcelle in the Learies Blonde series and Fiona in the romantic comedy A Cinderella Story. Coolidge is also a regular actor in Christopher Guest's fantasy films, such as Best in Show, A Mighty Wind, For Your Consideration and Mascots. Coolidge has appeared in films including Click, Date Movie, Epic Movie, American Dreamz, Soul Men, Gentlemen Broncos, Austenland, Like a Boss, and Promising Young Woman. On television, she was known for her roles as Roberta "Bobbie" Morganstern in NBC sitcom Joey, Betty in ABC teen drama Family Life of the American Teenager, and Zofia "Sophie" Kaczyński in CBS sitcom 2 Broke. Girls. Coolidge is a former actor with The Groundlings, an impromptu Los Angeles-based comedy and skit troupe. In 2001, she appeared in the movie Pootie Tang.
