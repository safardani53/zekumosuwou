From Taylor Swift to Jeanne Cadieu, who has Jake Gyllenhaal dated?

SINCE his rise to fame, movie star Jake Gyllenhaal had a series of high-profile romances with musicians, actresses, and models alike.

Prior to dating Jeanne Cadieu, Jake's dating portfolio includes a number of A-list celebrities - including Taylor Swift, Reese Witherspoon, and Rachel McAdams.

Who has Jake Gyllenhaal dated?

The public saw Jake Gyllenhaal's star rise during the 2000s after starring in a string of successful films.

The star actor had roles in Donnie Darko, Brokeback Mountain, Love & Other Drugs, and Spider-Man: Far From Home.

Similar to his career moves, Jake's personal romances raise just as many headlines.

Here's a look at the Oscar-nominee's star-studded dating history:

Jenny Lewis

Rewind all the way back to 2001, and 21-year-old Jake was dating singer-songwriter Jenny Lewis.

They were both starting out in their careers and their relationship was brief.

But they remained friends, and Jake even brought Jenny as his date to the 2011 Golden Globes ceremony.

Kirsten Dunst

Jake was introduced to Hollywood star Kirsten Dunst when she was working with his sister Maggie on Mona Lisa Smile.

They began dating in 2002 and soon moved in together - even adopting a German Shepherd named Atticus. 

However, by 2004, the pair split, and Jake kept custody of their dog, who died in 2015.

Speaking to the News of the World, Kirsten explained they parted ways over a difference in lifestyle preferences.

She said: "He’s a stay-at-home boy and I’m an out-on-the-town girl."

Natalie Portman

Jake reportedly briefly dated Marvel star Natalie Portman in 2006.

The pair were spotted out on a dinner date, but their romance didn't last long.

They starred together in the 2009 film Brothers.

Reese Witherspoon

Between March 2007 and December 2009, Jake had an on-off relationship with Legally Blonde star Reese Witherspoon.

Reese - who is now happily married to talent agent Jim Toth - was in the middle of her divorce from Ryan Phillippe when she and Jake met.

The pair split for the final time in 2009 but remain good friends.

In a 2016 episode of The Jess Cagle Interview, Jake spoke of his admiration for Reese.

He said: "She is, as I would say about most of the women who I'm close friends with or had relationships in my life, one of the smartest, strongest, people I've ever met."

Taylor Swift

Taylor Swift and Jake started dating in 2010 after reportedly meeting at a dinner party held at the London home Gwyneth Paltrow, shared with then-husband Chris Martin.

They were then spotted together backstage at a taping of Saturday Night Live in October 2010.

But after spending the holidays apart, Taylor and Jake reportedly split early in 2011.

Following the release of Taylor's 2021 'Red' album, Jake started trending as many Swifties believe her song, All Too Well, which was made into a 10-minute short film, is about their former relationship.

Anna Kendrick

Another actress Jake is rumored to have dated is Pitch Perfect sensation, Anna Kendrick.

The pair were linked together while shooting the action thriller movie End of Watch in 2011.

But rumors were never confirmed.

Alyssa Miller

Model Alyssa Miller began dating Jake in 2013

They were first rumored to be dating after being snapped snogging in a New York City coffee shop.

But their fling was short-lived and reportedly fizzled out within a year.

Rachel McAdams

Back in 2014, rumors swirled that Mean Girls star Rachel McAdams was dating Jake.

At the time, the pair were filming the sports drama Southpaw.

They were pictured going for cozy dinners and even attended boxing matches together.

But they both denied rumors about them dating and insisted they were just good friends.

Ruth Wilson

After dismissing rumors about dating Rachel, Jake was linked to actress Ruth Wilson.

He was caught kissing her in 2015 after a workout class in NYC.

They were working on Broadway show Constellations at the time, but never confirmed dating rumors.

Jeanne Cadieu

Since 2018, Jake has been loved up with his current girlfriend Jeanne Cadieu.

He has a 16 year age difference with model Jeanne, but the pair have been together almost three years.

They were first photographed together in Paris in December 2018.

Speaking to Vogue in May 2020, he said: "Seeing life as something that is, you know, fleeting, and the world being as it is now.

"I've turned to my family, I've turned to my friends and I've turned to love. I'm a little less interested in the work, I would say, and more interested in that."

In February 2022, the actor told Esquire in another interview: "In a lot of ways, we're family. I'm in a relationship that is full of love and support. I feel so at ease."

"I'm not unaware that there's interest in my life. My life is wonderful."

The star added about his model significant other: "I have a relationship that is truly wonderful, and I have a family I love so much. And this whole period of time has made me realize that."

This was their first appearance together since they attended the release of Maggie Gyllenhaal's motion picture, The Lost Daughter, in September 2021.
