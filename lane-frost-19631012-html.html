Lane Frost (Bull Rider) - Age, Birthday, Bio, Facts, Family, Net Worth, Height &amp; More

Lane Clyde Frost was an American professional bull rider who was the 1987 World Champion of the Professional Rodeo Cowboys Association and a 1990 ProRodeo Hall of Fame inductee. He was the only rider to score qualified rides from the 1987 World Champion and 1990 ProRodeo Hall of Fame bull Red Rock. He died in the arena at the 1989 Cheyenne Frontier Days Rodeo as a result of injuries sustained when the bull Takin' Care of Business struck him after the ride.
