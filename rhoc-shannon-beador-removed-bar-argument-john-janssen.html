RHOC's 'wasted' Shannon Beador 'removed from bar' after 'screaming' at ex John Janssen's daughter

RHOC star Shannon Beador had been asked to leave a bar after an argument broke out.

The incident occurred earlier this month at a Tiki Bar in Costa Mesa, California.

Shannon, 59, was joined by her ex boyfriend, John Janssen and his family, as reported by PageSix.

Later on in the night, Shannon got angry and started "screaming" at John's daughter.

A witness told an Instagram account @igfamousbydana that the Real Housewives of Orange County star looked to be "wasted" and "had to be held back and removed by security."

However, it was later revealed that the security guards were helping Shannon find her Uber to get home safely.

The witness also claimed that John and his family "remained calm" during the argument.

The news outlet revealed that in many photos, men wearing security uniforms take out an "upset and disheveled" Shannon off the property.

A source close to the Real Housewives star confirmed that there was an "argument" among several people and "everyone was screaming at each other."

John spoke to the outlet: "Yes, there was an argument, but it was at the end of the night, the lights were up, and everyone was being asked to leave."

THEIR RELATIONSHIP

Shannon and John met through mutual friends after her messy divorce from ex-husband David Beador in 2019.

In July of that year, they then were public with their relationship.

Over the years, Shannon gushed about her new man, which is why many fans were shocked when they split in 2023.

At the time, John told People that he broke up with the reality star because he didn't see a future with her but stressed that it was a "super hard" decision to make.

"I've been in pain over it," he told the outlet. "It's sad because I love Shannon very much. I have loved her more deeply than any woman in my life. She's one of a kind; so funny and generous and full of life and adventurous. We have a connection that's unlike anything I've ever had. And I know that for as long as I live, I'll never meet anyone like her again.

"But that doesn't mean we're right for each other for the rest of our lives."

He continued: "It doesn't make sense to be together if you don't believe in it.

"The last thing I wanted to do is hurt Shannon, but I do think I did the right thing for the both of us.

"We're two people who love each other very much, but love is not always enough. And while I know Shannon sees it as I've said things to her I never meant, I hope she'll be able to see that there's no bad person here. It's two really good people who couldn't make it work."

'BLINDSIDED'

Shannon also spoke with PEOPLE following the split, revealing that she was "blindsided by the breakup."

"All I can do is repeat in my head what he said to me while we were filming," Shannon said.

"We were in a great place. But seven days later, when the cameras were down, it was a completely different story. He told me he was done with the relationship. And to hear what he said to me then was absolutely devastating.

"I've never loved anyone more in my life," she continued.

"I was hopeful this was the man I was going to spend the rest of my life with. I wanted it to work out. I've never loved anyone like I've loved John. But clearly, he didn't feel the same way."
