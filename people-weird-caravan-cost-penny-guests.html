A WOMAN has revealed the “weirdest” part of their home is that they have a caravan in her back garden.

Morgan Bold uploaded a video giving a tour around the static house and saying they use it for guests and parties, and it didn’t cost her a penny.

In a clip which has racked up over 14,000 likes, she said: “That one thing that’s so weird to everyone else but normal for us.

“Anyone else have a caravan in their garden?

“It was basically my aunty’s when we were renovating her house, because she lives with us.

“So no one lives there at the moment but we just have it for guests and when we have parties.

More on homes

“We’ve done it all up inside.”

Morgan, who posts under @morgan.bold, then showed inside the home, which included a kitchen, a lounge with a sofa bed and a bathroom with a bathtub.

The caravan also has two bedrooms, one with a double bed and the other with two beds.

She added: “If that isn’t boujee I don’t know what is.”

Her favourite part of the caravan is the decking at the back, which has a hot tub and seating area.

And the area leads to her main garden, which has a BBQ and firepit for parties. 

Morgan continued: “It’s just chef’s kiss.”

Many people were highly impressed with the caravan, and many people said they should rent it out for money.

One said: “Put it on Airbnb - earn a bit.”

Morgan replied: “we’ve actually talked about this before! Maybe it’s time we do it.”

Another added: “Wow, incredible.”
