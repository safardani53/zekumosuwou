Hangout Festival 2022: confirmed line-up revealed

THE HANGOUT Festival returns for 2022 and the final line-up is released ranging from hip hop and pop to psychedelic rock and country.

From May 20-May 22, fans can flock to the beaches of Gulf Shores in Alabama for three days of fun, sun, and music.

What is Hangout Festival?

The Hangout Festival typically takes place every year at the Gulf Shores in Alabama.

Accompanying the popular bar, The Hangout, this festival presents a number of A-list and up-and-coming artists to perform for the masses over three days.

The festival was first founded by A.J. Niland, Shaul Zislin, and creative director of the festival, Lilly Zislin in 2009 and has since grown to garner national attention.

This will be the first time the festival is being held since 2019 after the Covid-19 pandemic forced the event to cancel in 2020 and 2021.

How can I get tickets?

The festival is open to all ages, but anyone 17 years and younger must be accompanied by an adult over the age of 21.

Presale tickets will become available on December 10, 2021 at 10am ET, but those wishing to purchase tickets early will need to pre-register on the Hangout Music Festival website.

Ticket prices start at $339 plus tax but for those who need extra time to pay, there is a payment plan option that requires 10 percent down and breaks the remaining cost into five payments.

Shuttle bus passes will also be available to purchase on the website, alongside hotel and condo recommendations.
