GET ready to say “he’s behind you” and “oh yes it is” as pantomime season is upon us.

But when is Cinderella on at The Marlowe Theatre in Canterbury and how can you get tickets for the whole family? Here's the FULL lowdown...

When is Cinderella on at The Marlowe Theatre?

The show is from November 23, 2018 at The Marlowe Theatre in Canterbury and will run until January 6, 2019.

The show is on EVERY DAY except Mondays and Christmas Day.

Most days have a matinee and an evening performance - and there are THREE performances on Saturdays.

You can find the theatre at The Friars, Canterbury, Kent CT1 2AS.

What celebrities are starring in Cinderella at The Marlowe Theatre?

Here's who is in the star cast:

  • Sally Lindsay as Fairy Godmother
  • Harry Reid as Dandini
  • Ben Roddy as Ugly Sister
  • Lloyd Hollett as Ugly Sister
  • Phil Gallagher as Buttons
  • Oliver Watton as Prince Charming
  • Cara Dudgeon as Cinderlla
  • Patrick Clancy as Baron

How can you get tickets for Peter Pan at The Marlowe Theatre?

Tickets range from £44.25 to £10.75

(Ticket prices are subject to change)

Book tickets:

  • Through The Marlowe's website
  • By calling 01227 787787
  • In person at the theatre's Box Office - open Monday to Sunday from 9am until 6pm.
