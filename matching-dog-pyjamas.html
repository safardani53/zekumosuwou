You can now get matching pyjamas for you and your dog - and they're unbelievably cute

WE thought we'd seen it all when it comes to pets clothing, but this fashion brand which specialises in threads for our furry companions has taken it to a new level.

American-based clothing retailer Fabdog is selling matching pyjama sets for you AND your dog.

The pair of you will look like the mutt's nuts with these cosy, plaid flannel PJs, which come in two different colours.

Whether it's just you and your number one cuddle buddy, or you kit out the whole family, there's no doubt you'll have a holiday card to trump all holiday cards with your dog in matching pup's pyjamas.

The human unisex PJ bottoms come in sizes small to extra large and are 100 per cent cotton.

For the hounds, owners can choose from an eight inch to a 20 inch outfit, and they even come with mini pockets - giving you somewhere to store their treats.

The sets aren't cheap, retailing at £37.89 ($50), though thankfully the company ships worldwide.

If you can't wait to get your paws on a pair, frustratingly (yet understandably) the adorable sets have currently sold out on the website.

But the brand told Pretty52 that they're getting more stock in very soon.

At the moment you can pre-order a pair that have an estimated shipping date of February 1 - perhaps the perfect Valentine's gift for your beloved four-legged friend?

Last month we told how B&M is selling Christmas outfits for dogs and prices start at just £3.99.

This followed the budget store flogging spooky Halloween costumes for pooches, and superhero pup costumes for £5.99.
