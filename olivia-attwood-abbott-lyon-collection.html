Olivia Attwood launches debut Abbott Lyon collection and it's packed with gorgeous pieces

OLIVIA Attwood has launched her debut collection with jewellery and accessories brand Abbott Lyon.

The new collection is packed with gorgeous designs that we can't get enough of.

Olivia X Abbott Lyon is a collection of designer-inspired pieces, that won't break the bank.

  • Olivia X Abbott Lyon, from £39 - buy here

The affordable collection is available from abbottlyon.com, and is priced from £39 - with prices ranging to £139.

The 30 piece collection includes pieces that elevate day to evening looks, and include gorgeous personalise details such as zodiac signs, initials, nameplates, and birthstones.

The collection includes 10 bestselling styles and 20 exclusive items, from necklaces, earrings, bangles and pendants.

Perfect for an on-trend layered look, or for minimal styling; we'll struggle not to buy it all.

We're obsessed with hero pieces including  Gold Bar Fine Chain (£59.00), Personalised Coin Fine Chain (£59.00), Zodiac Necklace, (£49.00), the Birthstone Signature Name Necklace (£79.00) and Crystal Heart Bangle (£49.00).

Speaking about the collection Olivia Attwood said: ‘I’ve worked with Abbott Lyon for the last few years, and I just love everything the brand does and what they stand for. When they approached me to do my own edit, I was absolutely thrilled – it’s a dream come true!

"I’m known for my love of designer jewellery, but it is such an expensive habit. I think Abbott Lyon is a great alternative and I adore that you can personalise the pieces - it makes their products so much more meaningful and special.

"I’m so excited to share this collection and hope everyone loves it as much as I do!"

You can shop the full Olivia X Abott Lyon collection at abbottlyon.com here.
